ABOUT US

Osos Group was established in 2002 by merging several engineering offices of different specializations and broad experience in engineering fields for more than twenty years.Osos Group has four main departments: Construction, Consulting, Electro-mechanics and Water/ Environment. Osos Group places people at the core of all developments and focuses on strengthening personal proficiencies and organisational capacities on all levels. Currently more than a 150 professional engineers work for Osos Group in different sections: project planning designing and execution. The consulting department is associated with professionals in various engineering fields providing services in Syria and recently in other countries. Osos Group specialist experience in all departments enables it to provide a wide range of services to various stakeholders involved in the development process on national and international levels. On the water sector, Osos Group Water/Environment Department focuses on sustainable rural development and sustainable water management so the acting plan is set to associate with well known international organizations that have extensive experience in water management and water policies when applying for related projects. Osos Group is capable to support the implementation process of its own studies under a request by providing a widespread expertise in project management and can offer expertise in evaluating approaches, programmes and processes as well as in quality assurance and monitoring.
